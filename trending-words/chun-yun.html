春运

Chūn yùn

Spring Festival Travel Rush

Meaning:

Refers to the annual travel frenzy during the Chinese New Year when millions of people return to their hometowns to reunite with family.

Cultural Context:

Known as the world's largest human migration, it highlights the importance of family in Chinese culture. It also reflects the challenges of China's massive population and infrastructure demands during this period.

Common Phrases:

  • 一路平安 (yí lù píng ān)

    Safe travels
